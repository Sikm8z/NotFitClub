About NotFit

As a couple of novice lifters, Margarito Hill and George Reyes founded NotFit to support non-lifters on their weightlifting journeys.

Founded in November 2022, NotFit employs coaches to provide instructional programs and coaching services to our members.

We also host some free content for intrigued lifters looking to explore and get started.

The Goal

Our mission at NotFit is to make lifting accessible to all people, by providing conent that is accesisble for all ages and adaptible to all abilities.
